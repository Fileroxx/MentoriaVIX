WHAT IS ‘MENTORIA VIX’?

Mentoria VIX is a mentoring company headquartered in Vitória.

Our team is made of experienced mentors with vast international experience who decided to share their journeys with whoever wants to hear, and love refueling by listening to other people’s stories. Instead of implementing some “How Tos”, we want to hold hands and invite for a walk.

Mentoria = Mentoring, in Portuguese. VIX = Airport IATA code for Vitória, ES,
the most beautiful city on the coast of Brazil and yet a true cradle of talents.
