Welcome To Mentoria VIX

About us

Mentoria = Mentoring, in Portuguese. VIX = Airport IATA code for Vitória, ES, the most beautiful city on the coast of Brazil and yet a true cradle of talents.

Mentoria VIX is a mentoring company headquartered in Vitória, Brazil. Our team is made of experienced mentors with vast international experience who decided to share their journeys with whoever wants to hear, and love refueling by listening to other people’s stories. Instead of implementing some “How Tos”, we want to hold hands and invite for a walk.

We are people helping people. We are dreamer chasing dreamers. So let’s go!

Our Services

Programs


Engage 2.2: Corporate Mentoring Program


According to Gallup's recent State of the Global Workplace report, 85% of employees are not engaged or actively disengaged at work. Why is that? Some tend to think people will just leave a team because someone else offered them a better pay. No. That is not the main reason. Through our Engage Mentoring program, your team will stick because they are growing!

One-on-one Development and Career Mentoring


Do you feel stuck in your own career? Challenged by decisions that should not even be that hard? Consult us on our one-on-on program. Our team of specialists will be happy to help you find out what the next step is. Truly, we have been through this,and we can help!

Thrive: ADHD as an asset


Many will refer to ADHD as a disease or a limitation of some sort. We choose to think that some of the world shakers in history were ADHD patients. Designed by a successful ADHD patient, our specialized program can help you find the best version of yourself. Corporate coaching available, consult us.


Non-Profit Mentoring and Networking


Helping the poor and transforming society is a noble choice that many have been embracing. However, it does require strategies and concepts that are very similar to the ones used in the business world. Part of our team is specialized in mentoring non-governmental initiatives.

Focused Giving


Giving is a great honor. Healthy companies give back to their communities. It is possible, however, to organize your company’s generosity impact. Our program will help you focus on the right priority and customize the social impact of your business initiative.

Customized Mentoring


Not to brag, but… we have specialized mentors for different areas of professional and academic career. Ask us. We will be happy to help!
